识别

Full

  • Request

    • Method: POST
    • Headers
      • Content-Type: application/json
    • URL: /Mark12306Captcha/api/v1.0/mark

      • {"originCaptcha":"captcha base64 string"}

  • Response

    • Headers
      • Content-Type: application/json
    • Body

    • ids 图片的编号
